# DrapixAI Pre-Launch Partner Brief (Fashion Ecommerce)

## What DrapixAI Does
DrapixAI helps fashion ecommerce brands add AI-powered virtual try-on for upper-body garments through a controlled, pilot-first rollout.

## Why This Matters For Your Brand
- Improves shopper confidence at product-page level.
- Reduces uncertainty before purchase.
- Gives your team a measurable pilot before committing larger budget.
- Adds a modern, conversion-focused experience without rebuilding your storefront.

## How We Help You Grow
- Faster testing loop:
  - Run pilot products first, measure engagement and conversion behavior, then scale.
- Cleaner operational flow:
  - Your team gets guidance from garment upload to confirmed catalog mapping.
- Lower commercial risk:
  - Start with trial usage and move to paid volume only after quality validation.

## Pilot Rollout Plan
1. Kickoff and success criteria definition.
2. Upload upper-body garment assets.
3. Import/scan product catalog for discovery context.
4. Confirm suggested garment-product mappings.
5. Run internal try-on preview.
6. Controlled storefront rollout on approved products.

## Integration Scope
- Browser SDK and REST API support.
- One production domain per public plan.
- Analytics visibility via dashboard.

## Current Commercial Snapshot
- Trial: up to 300 try-ons over 12 days.
- Starter: 1,000 upper-body try-ons/month.
- Growth: 5,000 upper-body try-ons/month.
- Enterprise: custom volume and onboarding.

## What We Need From You
- Brand/store URL.
- Product catalog sample.
- 10 to 30 upper-body garment assets for pilot.
- Team owner for ecommerce ops + technical setup.
