# DrapixAI Enterprise Motion Playbook (Separate Track)

## Purpose
Handle large/populated fashion brands with a separate, higher-touch approach.

## Important Rule
- Do not include enterprise accounts in growth-store outreach batches.
- Enterprise outreach is founder-led and approval-gated at every step.
- Use `marketing/enterprise-leads-template.csv` as the active enterprise account sheet.

## Enterprise ICP Signals
- Multi-country or large multi-category catalog.
- Complex approval chain (founder, ecommerce head, product, security, procurement).
- Preference for custom contracts and implementation assurance.

## Enterprise Positioning
- Focus on controlled pilot governance, reliability, and rollout process quality.
- Discuss operational fit, team enablement, and phased rollout support.
- Avoid high-volume outbound cadence used for growth stores.

## Enterprise Outreach Method
1. Build a named-account list (10 to 20 max at a time).
2. Prioritize warm intros, partner channels, and executive mapping.
3. Use personalized outreach with account-specific context.
4. Run discovery call before demo commitment.
5. Prepare custom pilot scope and stakeholder map.

## Enterprise Messaging Angle
- "We help large fashion teams de-risk AI try-on rollout with phased pilot controls."
- "Start with a tightly scoped upper-body pilot, then expand only after internal quality and workflow validation."

## Enterprise KPI (Track B)
- Warm conversations started.
- Discovery calls completed.
- Multi-stakeholder pilot proposals sent.
- Enterprise pilots moved to evaluation.

## Do Not Reuse Without Adaptation
- Growth-store cold email sequence.
- High-volume send cadence.
- Generic CTA-only messaging.
