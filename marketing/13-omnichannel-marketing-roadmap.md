# DrapixAI Omnichannel Marketing Roadmap (Solo Founder)

Date: 2026-04-30
Scope: Pre-launch to early scale (90 days)
Rule: Email is one channel, not the strategy.

## 1) North Star and KPI Framework
- North Star: Qualified pilot calls booked per week.
- Supporting KPIs:
  - Top funnel: website demo starts, inbound inquiries, outbound reply rate, content reach.
  - Mid funnel: pilot calls booked, trial activations, time-to-first successful try-on.
  - Bottom funnel: trial-to-paid conversion, first-month retained accounts.

## 2) Channel Mix (Omnichannel)
- Channel A: Founder-led content (LinkedIn + short video)
- Channel B: Outbound (email + LinkedIn DM follow-through)
- Channel C: Website conversion funnel (demo -> pilot call CTA)
- Channel D: Lifecycle nurture (trial onboarding + activation nudges)
- Channel E: Partnerships (agencies, Shopify experts, ecommerce consultants)
- Channel F: Community and authority (webinars, founder POV posts, case snapshots)
- Channel G: Paid retargeting (small budget, warm audiences only)
- Channel H: Enterprise named-account motion (separate playbook and CSV)

## 3) 90-Day Roadmap
### Phase 1 (Days 1-30): Foundation + Signal Capture
- Build predictable weekly publishing rhythm:
  - 3 short videos/week
  - 4 founder posts/week
- Run growth-store outbound in controlled batches (10 to 25 per batch).
- Add conversion CTA in every content piece: "Book a 20-minute pilot setup."
- Instrument basic dashboard tracking:
  - traffic source
  - demo starts
  - pilot call requests
- Launch one monthly webinar landing concept (draft + waitlist).

### Phase 2 (Days 31-60): Pipeline Expansion + Conversion Lift
- Scale qualified lead additions (global + India growth-store mix).
- Add segmented nurture:
  - no-reply outbound follow-up
  - demo-user nurture
  - trial-not-activated nudges
- Publish first proof-style story:
  - problem
  - pilot setup
  - outcome snapshot
- Start partner lane:
  - 10 agency/consultant outreach attempts per week.

### Phase 3 (Days 61-90): Systemization + Enterprise Readiness
- Stabilize weekly operating cadence and KPI reviews.
- Introduce warm retargeting campaigns (small budget tests).
- Build enterprise-specific pitch pack and stakeholder map process.
- Keep growth and enterprise motions fully separate in execution.

## 4) Weekly Execution Template (Solo Founder)
- Monday:
  - Lead research + segmentation refresh
  - Publish 1 founder post
- Tuesday:
  - Batch outbound send (growth track)
  - Draft and queue 1 short video
- Wednesday:
  - Publish educational video + CTA post
  - Trial activation follow-up review
- Thursday:
  - Partner outreach + relationship follow-ups
  - Enterprise account mapping (named account prep only)
- Friday:
  - KPI review
  - Lessons learned
  - Next week planning and approval queue prep

## 5) Channel-Specific Plays
### Founder Content
- Goal: trust and inbound.
- Format: operator insights, product walkthroughs, onboarding mistakes, pilot checkpoints.

### Outbound
- Goal: consistent pilot pipeline.
- Motion: quality-first lead targeting, small controlled batches, reply-focused follow-up.

### Website Funnel
- Goal: convert traffic to pilot calls.
- Priority assets:
  - clear hero value proposition
  - social proof snippets
  - visible pilot CTA
  - demo-to-call prompt

### Lifecycle Nurture
- Goal: increase activation from signed-up trials.
- Trigger moments:
  - signup complete
  - no try-on run in 24h
  - partial setup
  - trial end window

### Partnerships
- Goal: lower-cost pipeline via intermediaries.
- Targets: agencies handling fashion ecommerce growth and Shopify implementation.

### Community/Authority
- Goal: credibility and warm demand.
- Tactics: monthly webinar, tactical founder posts, educational threads, case snapshots.

### Paid Retargeting
- Goal: convert warm visitors.
- Start only with:
  - site visitors
  - pricing page visitors
  - demo users
- Keep budget controlled until message-market signal is stable.

### Enterprise Motion
- Goal: strategic larger accounts.
- Use only:
  - `marketing/enterprise-leads-template.csv`
  - `marketing/11-enterprise-motion-playbook.md`
- Never mix enterprise outreach style with growth-store cadences.

## 6) Approval-Gated Decision Points
- Any new channel launch.
- Any budget increase.
- Any messaging change affecting claim scope.
- Any move from growth-only batches to enterprise touches.

## 7) First 2-Week Sprint (Ready To Execute)
- Week 1:
  - Add 40 new growth leads.
  - Send 2 growth outreach batches of 15 each (after approval).
  - Publish 2 short videos + 3 founder posts.
  - Set up trial activation follow-up checklist.
- Week 2:
  - Add 40 more growth leads.
  - Send 2 follow-up waves.
  - Publish 1 educational walkthrough video + 3 founder posts.
  - Run first KPI review and iterate messaging.

## 8) Success Criteria by Day 30
- 8 to 12 qualified pilot calls booked.
- 3 to 5 active pilot conversations.
- Repeatable weekly cadence maintained for 4 consecutive weeks.
