# Additional Growth Experiments For DrapixAI

## 1) Partner-Led Distribution
- Build a partner tier for Shopify agencies and ecommerce consultants.
- Give partners a co-branded pilot deck + referral incentive.
- Run monthly partner office hours for implementation Q&A.

## 2) Conversion-Focused Landing Pages
- Create vertical pages:
  - "Virtual Try-On for Women's DTC Brands"
  - "Virtual Try-On for Streetwear Stores"
  - "Virtual Try-On for Modest Fashion Ecommerce"
- Add short demo clips + pilot CTA above the fold.

## 3) Demo-to-Call Funnel
- Add in-app prompt after successful demo try-on:
  - "Want this on your live products? Book pilot setup."
- Trigger follow-up email sequence for demo users who do not register.

## 4) Founder-Led Outbound
- Send personalized Loom-style mini audits:
  - 60-second review of a brand's product page experience.
- Offer direct pilot recommendation based on their catalog type.

## 5) Retargeting Stack
- Pixel audiences:
  - Pricing page viewers
  - Demo users
  - Contact page visitors
- Ads:
  - 15s reminder creatives with trial CTA
  - Partner cohort urgency

## 6) Proof Assets
- Build case-story format:
  - Baseline metrics
  - Pilot setup timeline
  - Result summary
  - Rollout decision
- Publish one proof asset every 2 weeks once pilots start.

## 7) Community + Events
- Host a monthly webinar:
  - "How to run AI try-on pilots without hurting conversion flow."
- Join ecommerce/fashion founder communities and share tactical clips.

## 8) Lifecycle Email
- Segment by intent:
  - New signup
  - Demo completed
  - Trial inactive
  - Trial near expiry
- Send role-based copy:
  - Founder, ecommerce manager, technical lead.

## 9) Content Repurposing Engine
- One long-form explainer -> 5 short clips -> 10 carousels -> 1 email.
- Keep weekly consistency over one-off campaigns.

## 10) Sales Assist Motions
- Build a "pilot calculator" sheet:
  - expected traffic
  - expected try-on usage
  - plan recommendation
- Use this in outbound replies to move from interest to qualified pipeline.
