# DrapixAI Video Production Kit

This kit turns each script into publishable assets quickly.

## 1) Fast Production Workflow
1. Pick one script from `02-video-scripts.md`.
2. Record clean voiceover (phone mic is acceptable if room is quiet).
3. Capture UI clips from demo/dashboard.
4. Add captions + brand colors.
5. Export 9:16 (Reels/Shorts) and 1:1 (LinkedIn feed).

## 2) Scene Prompt Templates (For AI Video Tools)

Use these prompts for scene generation in tools like Runway/Sora/Pika.

### Prompt A: Fashion Ecommerce Pain
"Cinematic ecommerce environment, online clothing shopper hesitating on product page, neutral studio lighting, modern UI overlays showing drop in conversion, realistic motion, 4k commercial style, clean typography space."

### Prompt B: DrapixAI Solution Sequence
"Modern SaaS dashboard walkthrough, garment upload cards, product mapping flow, AI virtual try-on preview results, polished startup promo look, blue-cyan accent lighting, smooth camera pans, 4k."

### Prompt C: Conversion Outcome
"Happy ecommerce team reviewing analytics uplift on laptop screens, fashion brand office environment, clean premium visuals, confidence and momentum tone, natural motion blur, 4k."

## 3) Editing Specs
- Duration targets:
  - Hook ads: 15 to 25 seconds
  - Explainer: 45 to 60 seconds
- Ratios:
  - 9:16 for Reels/Shorts
  - 1:1 for LinkedIn feed
  - 16:9 for website embed
- Caption style:
  - Bold sans text, high contrast, 2 lines max.
- Pace:
  - New cut every 1.5 to 3 seconds for short-form.

## 4) Visual Checklist
- Mention upper-body scope explicitly.
- Show real product flow: upload -> discovery -> suggested match -> confirm -> preview.
- End with one CTA only.
- Keep logo visible in first 5 seconds and final 3 seconds.

## 5) CTA Variants
- "Book your 20-min pilot setup."
- "Start your 300 try-on trial."
- "Join pre-launch partner cohort."

## 6) Weekly Publishing Cadence
- Monday: Problem-solution short

- Friday: Offer/CTA ad
- Sunday: Founder insight clip
