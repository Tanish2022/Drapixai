# DrapixAI Marketing Video Scripts (Ready To Use)

Use these as short-form videos for LinkedIn, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts.

## Video 1: 30s Brand Intro
- Goal: Awareness
- Format: Founder voice + product UI + try-on results

### Script
- Hook (0-4s):
  - Voiceover: "Fashion shoppers leave when they cannot picture the fit. DrapixAI fixes that."
  - On-screen text: "AI Virtual Try-On for Fashion Ecommerce"
- Problem (4-10s):
  - Voiceover: "Most brands lose sales because product photos alone do not answer 'Will this look right on me?'"
  - On-screen text: "Confidence gap = lost conversions"
- Solution (10-22s):
  - Voiceover: "DrapixAI lets your team upload garment assets, map products, and launch upper-body virtual try-on with a controlled rollout."
  - On-screen text: "Upload -> Map -> Preview -> Launch"
- CTA (22-30s):
  - Voiceover: "Join our pre-launch partner cohort and run your pilot this month."
  - On-screen text: "Start 300 free trial try-ons"
