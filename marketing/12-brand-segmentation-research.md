# Brand Segmentation Research (Growth vs Enterprise)

Date: 2026-04-30

## Objective
Create two separate targeting lists for DrapixAI:
- Growth Stores: faster pilot-friendly DTC and scaling ecommerce stores.
- Enterprise Brands: large/populated brands and marketplaces requiring a custom enterprise motion.

## Segmentation Logic Used
- Growth Store classification:
  - DTC-heavy or scale-up ecommerce brands.
  - Usually single-brand or narrower catalog focus.
  - Better fit for faster pilot cycles and shorter sales loops.
- Enterprise classification:
  - Multi-market retailers/marketplaces or large multi-brand platforms.
  - Public-company or large corporate operating model indicators.
  - Typically needs multi-stakeholder, founder-led enterprise sales motion.
- Founder override:
  - Snitch is treated as Enterprise by explicit founder direction.

## Outputs
- Growth list: `marketing/brand-leads-template.csv`
- Enterprise list: `marketing/enterprise-leads-template.csv`
